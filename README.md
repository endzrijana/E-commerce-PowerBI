E-commerce Sales Analysis - Power BI
Project Goal: To evaluate sales performance, logistics efficiency, and customer purchasing behavior across diverse geographical regions and timeframes.

Key Insights:
Regional Leadership: São Paulo is the primary sales hub with over 2.8K orders, significantly outpacing other major cities like Rio de Janeiro.

Payment Preferences: Credit cards are the dominant payment method, accounting for 73.84% of all transactions (28.27K orders).

Revenue Driver: The Furniture_Decor category is the top revenue generator, contributing 149.11K to the total income.

Order Trends: A strong upward trend in order volume was recorded throughout 2018, peaking in November with 2.9K orders.

Logistics Efficiency: Outlier analysis reveals that while most shipping charges remain stable, certain high-value items in categories like housewares and auto show significant variance in delivery costs.
